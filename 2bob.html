HISTORY


Bank of Baroda was incorporated on July 20 1908 as a as a private bank with the name The Bank of Baroda Ltd. The Bank was established with a paid up capital of Rs 1 million and was founded by Maharaja Sayajirao III of Baroda. In the year 1910 the Bank opened their first branch in the city of Ahmedabad.
